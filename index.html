ABOUT

In today's rapidly changing environment, programming skills are essential tools that can be utilized and incorporated into various fields and domains. Hence, it becomes absolutely essential to equip young minds with such skills. GEEKSMAN aims to establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding. The club's motto is to Create-Build-Innovate

OUTCOMES

GEEKSMAN helps members gain in-depth knowledge of coding. This helps them immensely during placements and can also bring laurels to the institution through various national and international programming contests .The club helps members develop an aptitude for logical thinking and visualizing a problem from various perspectives and creating multiple innovative solutions

REGISTRATION

Our club is open for all the Geeks enthusiastic in coding who want to give their skills a boost & ideas a shape.

Round 1:

Aptitude + Coding test:   A test consisting of some brain teasing questions to evaluate your analytical and logical thinking.

Round 2:

Project showcasing:   To test your knowledge and experience of Coding.

Round 3:

Personal Interview:   To check your confidence and communication skills.
